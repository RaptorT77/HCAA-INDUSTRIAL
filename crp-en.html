As a complete chain manufacturer, it develops and produces all critical components: controllers, servo motors, intelligent sensors, and complete robots. This vertical integration ensures total compatibility, direct technical support, and customization capability according to specific manufacturing requirements.

Complete Chain

Manufacturer with total vertical integration: in-house production of CRP controllers, servo motors, drivers, sensors, and complete robots. Quality control at every phase, from electronic design to final assembly. Technical customization capability and direct support from development engineering.

Specialization

"Start with welding, base on welding, more than welding". CROBOTP built its expertise by mastering the most demanding processes in industrial robotics: MIG/MAG, TIG, and spot welding. This mastery in critical applications ensures that its handling and assembly robots offer the same level of precision and reliability.
