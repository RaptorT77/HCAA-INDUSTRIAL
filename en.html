Virtual Commissioning

HCAA offers advanced Virtual Commissioning solutions, allowing the validation of automation systems and process behavior control in digital environments before physical implementation. This approach guarantees efficiency, precision, and reduction of times and costs in project commissioning.

Main services include:

  • Simulation of automated systems, replicating the interaction of PLCs, HMIs, sensors, and actuators in virtual platforms
  • Validation of control logic and operational sequences before real installation, allowing preventive adjustments in design
  • Reduction of downtime and rework through exhaustive testing in controlled environments
  • Integration tests with electrical, mechanical, and robotic systems, ensuring coherence between technical disciplines
  • Use of tools like Siemens, Rockwell Emulate, and CAD/CAE tools for realistic and connected simulation
  • Collaboration between engineering, programming, and operation teams, facilitating joint system validation
  • Training on virtual environments and simulation protocols, strengthening technical knowledge of involved personnel

Through our Virtual Commissioning approach, we accompany companies in their transition towards more agile, safe, and digitally prepared projects, aligning with Industry 4.0 principles.
